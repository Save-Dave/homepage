Our home is where we eat, sleep, and play. However, many of our actions at home are harming the environment. Making small attempts to help the environment will not only reduce costs and improve your health, but it will also be a step towards helping save the environment!
YOU CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE!
Turn off applicances when not in use.
Use showers over baths-showers use an average of 12 fewer gallons than baths.
Use recycled paper.
Do not pre-heat the oven.
Use one less paper napkin- if every person in the U.S. used one less napkin a day, more than a billion pounds of napkins could be saaved from landfills each year.
Brush without running-you can conserve up to 5 gallons per day if you stop.
