Welcome to Lifeland!
Nature is an important part of our world, where animals and plants survive. However, we are threatening it. Poaching is the illegal killing of animals, drastically shrinking the population of species. It can also refer to the illegal collection of plants, decreasing the population and variety in the species. In an effort to not lose forests, we fight to stop all forest fires. But for certain environments, it is more beneficial to let the fire run its course. Without wildfires, plants in those environments grow thick and tall, choking out smaller plants. In other areas, trees are cut down, killing entire forests, to provide lumber and land. This takes entire habitats away from thousands of species, leaving them homeless.
