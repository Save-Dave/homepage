Welcome to Oceana!
The ocean provides so much for so much for us-food, medicine, and oxygen. Today, the ocean is facing grave threats from pollutants that not only harm marine life but also directly impact humans.Millions of animals are harmed or killed from plastic trash, oil spills, hunting, and disruption of habitats.
YOU CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE!
Reduce the effects of climate change on the ocean by reducing your carbon footprint-use a bike to get around, switch to fluourescent light bulbs, and lower the thermostat a couple of degrees.
Use fewer plastic products-use a reusable water bottle, store food in nondisposale containers, and recycle whenever possible.
